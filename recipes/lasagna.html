Lasagna

A delicious plate of lasagna

This is a traditional lasagna recipe layered with a rich meat sauce and a creamy parmesan white sauce, plus the perfect amount of mozzarella cheese! No-boil lasagna noodles, layered with a mixture of quality store-bought marinara, mixed with ground beef cooked to perfection, ricotta, mozzarella, and a special parmesan mix - all garnished with fresh basil on top. It's comfort food at its best!

Ingredients

  • 12 no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 2 jars of quality store-bought marinara sauce
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain the fat.
  3. Add the marinara sauce to the skillet with the beef, stir well, and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  4. While the meat sauce is simmering, mix the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese, and the parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  5. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Arrange 4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. Spread a third of the cheese mixture over the noodles. Repeat these layers twice.
  7. Top the final layer of noodles with the remaining meat sauce, followed by the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. After 25 minutes, remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and starting to brown.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before cutting and serving. Garnish with fresh basil.
