Chicken Alfredo

Yummy chicken alfredo

This is a creamy and indulgent dish of fettuccine pasta tossed with a rich alfredo sauce made with butter, cream, and parmesan cheese. It's topped with tender and juicy slices of chicken seasoned with Italian herbs and spices. This is a classic Italian-American dish that's easy to make and perfect for a cozy dinner.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces Dry Fettuccine Pasta
  • 1 pound Skinless Chicken Breasts (boneless)
  • 1 ¾ teaspoons Italian Seasoning
  • ¾ teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • ½ teaspoon Pepper
  • 2 tablespoons Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Butter
  • ½ cup Butter (cut into large cubes or slices)
  • 2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 clove Garlic (minced)
  • ¾ teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • ¼ teaspoon Salt
  • 2 cups Parmesan Cheese (freshly grated)

Steps

  1. Cook the Fettuccine al dente, according to package instructions.
  2. Season the chicken, then pan-fry in oil and butter, for 6 minutes a side, or until an internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F. Transfer to a cutting board and let rest for a few minutes, then cut into 1/2-inch thick slices. Tent with foil, while you prepare the sauce.
  3. Using the same pan you used to cook the chicken, the cream, butter, and seasonings are incorporated and simmered on medium-low heat until thickened, then the Parmesan is added in until melted and smooth.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some of the liquid to loosen the sauce (only if necessary.) Toss immediately with the Alfredo sauce. Divide the pasta among serving bowls and top with a few slices of cooked chicken.
